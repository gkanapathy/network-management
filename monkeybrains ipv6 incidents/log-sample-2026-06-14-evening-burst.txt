HOW THE MONITORING WORKS (so these lines are interpretable):
The router runs ICMP probes every 10 seconds, in parallel, over the SAME
Monkeybrains link:
  - IPv6 full path : ping 2606:4700:4700::1111 (Cloudflare) sourced from our
                     delegated /56  -> "host: 2606:4700:4700::1111"
  - IPv4 first hop : ping 162.217.74.129 (our MB gateway)
  - IPv4 full path : ping 1.0.0.1 (Cloudflare), route-pinned out the MB link
A probe "down" = a full 3-packet round lost (each packet 2s timeout). Each
outage below lasts ~10s, i.e. recovers on the very next probe round.
